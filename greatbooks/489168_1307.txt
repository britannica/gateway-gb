Work: The Queen of Spades Field: queen of spades Author: Alexander Pushkin Title: CONCLUSION Hermann went out of his mind. He is now confined in room Number 17 of the Obukhov Hospital. He never answers any questions, but he constantly mutters with unusual rapidity: “Three, seven, ace! Three, seven, queen!” Lizaveta Ivanovna has married a very amiable young man, a son of the former steward of the old Countess. He is a civil servant, and has a considerable fortune. Lizaveta is bringing up a poor relative. Tomsky has been promoted to the rank of captain, and is marrying Princess Pauline.
